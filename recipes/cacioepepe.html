Cacio e pepe

This is a recipe that we have made in our family for many years--everyone loves it. It's a very basic and easy variation on mac 'n cheese.
Ingredients
  • 1/4 pound spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olve oil
  • 1/1 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
Steps
  1. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil
  2. Cook spaghetti and Drain it, keeping some of the cooking water
  3. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat
  4. Add garlic and pepper
  5. Cook and stiry until fragrant (1, 2 minutes)
  6. Add spaghetti and Pecorino Romano with saved cooking water
