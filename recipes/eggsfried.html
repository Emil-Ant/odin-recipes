Fried Eggs

This is my super simple sunny side up fried eggs recipe.
Ingredients
  • 3 Large organic eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Seasoning (salt and sumac/black pepper for example)
Steps
  1. Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat
  2. Crack the eggs into the pan
  3. Close the pan using a lid of appropriate size
  4. When egg whites are not transparent, season to preference
