Grilled Chicken

A super quick, simple and healthy chicken recipe
Ingredients
  • 2 medium sized chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, Black and white pepper, smoked paprika, oregano
Steps
  1. Cut each breast in half
  2. Add chicken to bowl
  3. Add 1 teaspon olive oil to chicken
  4. Add spices to chicken
  5. Marinade chicken for one hour
  6. Cook chicken by desired way (grill, pan fry, oven)
